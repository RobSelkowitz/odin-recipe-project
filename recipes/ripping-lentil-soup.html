Cranberry Sauce à la Bart

This classic dish combines the delightful tang of cranberries, the sweetness of sugar, and the jiggle of pectin. You, of course, need not worry yourself with any of the messy details. It's a recipe any pre-teen can handle.

You'll need:

  • One can jellied-style cranberry sauce
  • Can opener
  • Rimmed serving plate

Directions:

  1. Open one end of the can with the can opener
  2. Gently slide cranberry sauce onto serving plate. Expect satisfying pop and plop sounds as the sauce exits the can
  3. Present with a flourish and enjoy!

Pro Tips:

  • Just stick it in the refrigerator until ready to serve. The dish will retain it's signature tube shape longer.
  • Puncture the underside of the can before opening the lid makes it easier to pour out the sauce. But you lose the pop and plop effect.
