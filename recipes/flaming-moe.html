The Flaming Moe

"I don't know the scientific explanation, but fire made it good."

You'll need:

  • Remnants of assorted liquors and spirits
  • Love.
  • Krusty's Non Narkotik Kough Syrup. Grape flavor is traditional.
  • A blender.
  • Pint glasses or similar for serving.
  • A lit cigarette or other source of flame.

Directions:

  1. Pour assorted liquors and spirits into blender. You'll need a total of one pint worth per serving.
  2. Add two Tbs of love. Nah, just kidding. Someone was screwing with the gas chromatograph.
  3. Add a substantial glug of Kough Syrup per serving.
  4. Blend thoroughly.
  5. Pour into serving glasses.
  6. Check for blindness. This step is vital.
    • Take a small sip.
    • Wave hand in front of face.
    • If you cannot see your hand discard the drink and seek medical attention immediately. Otherwise, continue.
  7. Set beverage ablaze using cigarette ash or alternative, and serve while still flaming.

Pro Tips:

  • Grape is the traditional Kough Syrup flavor, but experiment until you find your favorite.
  • Keep a fire extinguisher and burn cream handy.
  • No, Aerosmith probably won't be in tonight.
